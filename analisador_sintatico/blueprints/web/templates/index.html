Gramática e conjuntos first e follow

S ::= aAb FIRST(S) = {a} FOLLOW(S) = {$}
A ::= aBc | dC FIRST(A)={a, d} FOLLOW(A)={b, c}
B ::= bC | & FIRST(B)={b, &} FOLLOW(B)={c}
C ::= dA | c FIRST(C)={d, c} FOLLOW(C)={b, c}
